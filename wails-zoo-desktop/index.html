üìñ About Horse Haven

Our Mission

At Horse Haven Farm, we are dedicated to the care, preservation, and celebration of these magnificent animals. Our farm has been a sanctuary for horses for over 25 years, providing them with love, proper care, and a forever home.

What We Do

  • Rescue and rehabilitate horses in need
  • Educational programs for horse enthusiasts
  • Therapeutic riding sessions
  • Breeding preservation of rare horse breeds
  • Community outreach and events

Our History

Founded in 1998 by the Thompson family, Horse Haven started with just two rescued horses. Today, we're home to over 30 horses, each with their own unique story and personality. Our 50-acre property provides ample space for grazing, training, and simply being a horse.
